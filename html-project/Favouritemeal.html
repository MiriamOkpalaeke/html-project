My Favourite Meal

Boiled Yam and Egg Sauce

Yam and egg are a delicious food combination. It is a popular food combo that is eaten in Nigeria and many parts of the world. The eggs are usually fried or made into egg sauce and eaten with boiled or fried yam.

Many people who eat yam and egg have often wondered if there are any benefits in eating these two foods together. The answer is yes, and there are many health benefits of yam and egg. They are both highly nutritious and versatile and can benefit your health in many ways.

The health benefits of yam and egg include improving cholesterol levels, eye health, and supporting weight loss.

These are ingredients needed to prepare the meal
# Ingredients Measurements
1 Yam 1/2 tuber
2 Egg 5 pieces
3 Tomatoes 3 pieces
4 Onions 1 bulb
5 Groundnut oil 1/2 small cup
6 Scotch bonnet 4 pieces
7 seasoning cube 1 cube
8 salt 1/2 tablespoon
9 curry and thyme 1/2 tablespoon each

How to Cook the Yam

  • Peel and slice the yam into small sizes
  • Rinse in clean water till the yam is clean
  • Put yam in a pan and add enough water to cover it
  • Add salt and place on medium heat and cook till it is soft
  • once soft drain out the excess water from the pot

How to cook the egg Sauce

  • Chop the tomatoes, onions and scotch bonnet into small pieces
  • Break eggs into a bowl and whisk till egg white and egg yolk are well combined.
  • add salt and whisk to combine then set it aside
  • Place a frying pan on a medium/low heat
  • Add of vegetable oil, heat for about 2-3 minutes
  • Add chopped peppers and onions and cook for about 4-5 minutes or till oil floats on the sauce
  • Add salt, seasoning, curry and thyme.
  • Stir to combine ingredient with sauce and also avoid sauce sticking to the pan.
  • Add whisked eggs to sauce and leave for about 3 minute. stir together and your sauce is ready
